Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Introduction

Ascend Level Up ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

Information We Collect

The App collects the following types of information:

  • Local Data: Your game progress, quests, stats, and profile information are stored locally on your device using browser storage.
  • Firebase Data: If you sign in, your game state is synchronized with our Firebase database for cloud backup.
  • Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand how users interact with the App. This includes device information, page views, and interactions.
  • Advertising: Google AdSense displays personalized ads based on your browsing activity.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the App's functionality
  • To understand user behavior through analytics
  • To display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense
  • To sync your progress across devices (with Firebase)

Data Storage & Security

Your local data is stored on your device. Cloud data is stored securely in Firebase servers. We implement standard security measures to protect your information.

Third-Party Services

The App uses:

  • Firebase (Google): For authentication and data storage
  • Google Analytics: For usage analytics
  • Google AdSense: For displaying advertisements

Your Rights

You can delete your local data by clearing your browser's storage or using the "Reset All Progress" button in the App. You can opt out of personalized ads through your Google Ad settings.
